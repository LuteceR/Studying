Death Stranding
Sam Bridges is a legendary porter and a former executive member of Bridges who played an integral role in expanding the Chiral Network and making the United Cities of America whole by embarking on a westward expedition.
Deadman was a doctor, researcher, and member of Bridges Medical Team who assisted Sam during his westward expedition. Due to his artificial nature, he had an obsession with the dead and BBs, along with a deep desire for connection and acceptance.
Cliff was a United States Army Special Forces Captain who was shot dead attempting to reclaim custody of his son from Bridges. Following his death, he became beached in a restless search for his "BB" as the "Combat Veteran".
Die-Hardman was the third and final President of the United Cities of America and formerly a Director of Bridges. He poses as Charlie in Death Stranding 2.
Bridget Strand was the former President of the United States of America, founder of Bridges and after the Death Stranding, the President of the United Cities of America up and until her death. She was the "ha" (body) of the sixth extinction entity, the "biological mother" of Samantha America Strand and the adoptive mother of Sam Strand.
Fragile was the young executive company and owner of the Fragile Express and an ally of Sam Porter during his expedition to west. She later became the founder of the civilian outfit Drawbridge.
